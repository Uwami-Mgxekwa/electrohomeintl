About ElectroHome International

ElectroHome International Pty Ltd is Johannesburg's trusted partner for all appliance repair and sales needs. Since 2009, we've been proudly serving people and businesses for 17 years, building a reputation for excellence and reliability in the appliance service industry.

Our team of certified technicians specializes in repairing all types of home appliances, from washing machines and refrigerators to ovens and dishwashers. We also offer a wide selection of new and quality second-hand appliances at competitive prices.

Whether you need a quick repair, a replacement appliance, or professional advice, ElectroHome International is here to help. We pride ourselves on fast, reliable service and customer satisfaction backed by nearly two decades of experience.
